This paper studies the coefficients

and the sums formed from them. The first part gives a recurrence for Φm,ε (λ; α), solves it, and then extracts finite reductions for all positive denominator powers. The product-binomial case α = 1 /R , especially 

α = 1 /4, gives finite identities for ordinary and alternating series. The cancellation identities follow from the same recurrence. The arbitrary-integral-slope extension treats denominators (dn + m + 1) K by separating
