## 摘要
我们提供了第10至第15个戴德金数（Dedekind Numbers）的高精度估计，其中 $D(10)$ 的精度达到4位，$D(15)$ 的精度达到2位。这些估计值是通过三种方法获得的，包括针对 $D(10)$ 的大量9维单调布尔函数的配对匹配，针对 $D(10)$、$D(11)$ 和 $D(12)$ 的参考子集法。此外，我们表现最好的方法“权重层分支”（Weight Layer Branching）为 $D(10)$ 到 $D(15)$ 提供了准确的估计，显著改进了 Korshunov 以及 Tian-Shun Chen 等人此前给出的最佳估计。arXiv:2606.09795

## Abstract
We provide highly accurate estimations of the 10th through 15th Dedekind Numbers, to a precision of 4 digits for $D(10)$, to 2 digits for $D(15)$. These estimates were obtained using three methods, including pair matching on large quantities of 9-dimensional monotone Boolean functions for $D(10)$, Reference Subsets for $D(10)$, $D(11)$, and $D(12)$. And our best method "Weight Layer Branching" which provided accurate estimates for all $D(10)$ through $D(15)$, strongly improving on the previous best known estimates by Korshunov and Tian-Shun Chen et al. arXiv:2606.09795
