## 摘要
具有 0<p<1 的非凸 ℓp 拟范数是一种强大的稀疏替代项，但使得近端算子 prox_{λ|·|^p} 的稳健评估变得非平凡。我们给出了所有 0<p<1 的标量近端映射的显式表征，包括阈值结构以及确保严格、孤立解的条件。将 Lagrange--Bürmann 反演应用于平稳方程，得到了较大正根的一致收敛级数，这为经典阈值之上提供了精确且数值稳定的公式。我们进一步推导了 Mellin--Barnes (MB) 积分表示，解释了其收敛半径并实现了经过认证的截断。基于这些要素，我们设计了一个“经过认证的混合评估器”（短级数 + 截断的垂直 MB 段），具有可计算的先验误差界限，在近阈值区域仍保持精确。对于有理数 p，高斯乘法公式将系数简化为移位 Gamma 函数的有限乘积，将级数重组为广义超几何函数的有限和，并解释了 p∈{1/3, 1/2, 2/3} 时的闭式解。我们将该评估器集成到具有不精确近端算谕（inexact proximal oracle）的近端梯度方法中，并在证书的标准可和性条件下证明了收敛性；MATLAB 实现和数值实验证实了其准确性，包括近阈值行为。

## Abstract
The nonconvex $\ell_p$ quasi-norm with $0<p<1$ is a powerful sparsity surrogate but makes the proximity operator $\mathrm{prox}_{λ|\cdot|^p}$ nontrivial to evaluate robustly. We give an explicit characterization of the scalar proximal map for all $0<p<1$, including the threshold structure and conditions ensuring strict, isolated solutions. Applying the Lagrange--Bürmann inversion to the stationarity equation yields a uniformly convergent series for the larger positive root, which provides an exact and numerically stable formula above the classical threshold. We further derive a Mellin--Barnes (MB) integral representation, explaining its radius of convergence and enabling certified truncation. Building on these ingredients, we design a {certified hybrid evaluator} (short series $+$ truncated vertical MB segment) with a computable a priori error bound that remains accurate in the near-threshold regime. For rational $p$, Gauss' multiplication formula reduces the coefficients to finite products of shifted Gamma functions, reorganizing the series into a finite sum of generalized hypergeometric functions and explaining the closed forms at $p\in\{1/3,1/2,2/3\}$. We integrate the evaluator into a proximal-gradient method with an inexact proximal oracle and prove convergence under standard summability of the certificates; MATLAB implementations and numerics confirm accuracy, including near-threshold behavior.
