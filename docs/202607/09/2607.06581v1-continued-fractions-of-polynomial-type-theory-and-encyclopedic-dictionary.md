## 摘要
在给出多项式型连分数的若干性质（特别是侧重于收敛性质和 Bauer-Muir-Apéry 加速技术）之后，我们提供了一份详尽的连分数列表，涵盖了特定的实数以及特殊函数。其中部分内容摘自多种不同来源，但大多数可能是全新的。除了提供这份列表外，我们的主要贡献之一是包含了这些连分数的精确收敛速度（有时仅精确到乘法常数），而这在现有文献中几乎总是被忽略。

## Abstract
After giving a number of properties of continued fractions of polynomial type, in particular focusing on convergence properties and Bauer-Muir-Apéry acceleration techniques, we give a large list of continued fractions, both for specific real numbers, and for special functions, some extracted from a number of different sources, but most others being probably new. In addition to providing such a list, one of our main additions is to include the exact speed of convergence of these continued fractions (sometimes only up to a multiplicative constant), which is almost always omitted in the literature.
