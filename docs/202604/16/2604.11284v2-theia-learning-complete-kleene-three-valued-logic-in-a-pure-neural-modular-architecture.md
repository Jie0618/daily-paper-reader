## 摘要
我们提出了 THEIA，这是一种模块化神经架构，能够在无需任何外部符号求解器的情况下端到端地学习完整的 Kleene 三值逻辑 (K3)，并研究了何种架构先验能够实现在不确定性下的组合泛化。THEIA 通过专用引擎处理四个数学领域（算术、排序、集合隶属关系、命题逻辑），这些引擎最终汇聚于一个逻辑模块。在包含 200 万个样本、输入空间约为 3.4 x 10^13 的数据集上进行训练，它在 5 个随机种子下均实现了 12/12 的 Kleene K3 规则覆盖，耗时 7.93 +/- 1.40 分钟（在匹配设置下快 6.5 倍；在 Transformer 标准调优下快约 3.6 倍，见附录 G）。一项模 3 序列组合实验实现了从 5 步训练到 500 步评估的泛化，准确率达 99.97% +/- 0.02% —— 这一结果需要结构化的骨干网络：在两种测试容量（0.80M 和参数匹配的 2.75M）下，将四引擎骨干网络替换为扁平 MLP 都会导致长度泛化在 50 步时下降至随机水平，而采用相同协议训练的 pre-LN TF8LTuned Transformer 基准模型（3,582,147 个参数）在 500 步时达到 99.24%（附录 F）。机制探针揭示了模块化诱导了一种“延迟判定”：上游引擎编码特定领域的变量，而不确定最终的真值（探针准确率 <= 74% 的仅不确定性上限），判定仅在逻辑引擎边界处产生 —— 这一点通过激活修补（activation patching）得到了因果证实（在 986 对匹配的 OR 对上翻转率为 100%，在 n=5 个种子上均得到复现；在 4,898 对上总计为 100.0%；并泛化至 AND，在 4,719 对上翻转率为 100%）。Transformer 基准模型通过一种性质截然不同的表示轨迹（先收缩后扩张）达到了同等的正确性，这表明模块化架构和整体式架构实现了不同的组合策略。

## Abstract
We present THEIA, a modular neural architecture that learns complete Kleene three-valued logic (K3) end-to-end without any external symbolic solver, and investigate what architectural prior enables compositional generalization under uncertainty. THEIA processes four mathematical domains (arithmetic, order, set membership, propositional logic) through dedicated engines that converge in a final logic module. Trained on a 2M-sample dataset with input space ~3.4 x 10^13, it achieves 12/12 Kleene K3 rule coverage across 5 seeds in 7.93 +/- 1.40 minutes (6.5x faster under matched settings; ~3.6x under Transformer-standard tuning, App. G). A mod-3 sequential composition experiment generalizes from 5-step training to 500-step evaluation at 99.97% +/- 0.02% -- a result requiring a structured backbone: replacing the four-engine backbone with a flat MLP collapses length generalization to chance by 50 steps at both tested capacities (0.80M and parameter-matched 2.75M), while a pre-LN TF8LTuned Transformer baseline (3,582,147 params) trained under the identical protocol reaches 99.24% at 500 steps (Appendix F). Mechanistic probing reveals that modularity induces a delayed verdict: upstream engines encode domain-specific variables without committing to the final truth value (probe accuracy <= 74% uncertainty-only ceiling), with the verdict emerging only at the Logic Engine boundary -- causally confirmed by activation patching (100% flip rate on 986 matched OR pairs, replicated across n=5 seeds; 100.0% aggregate on 4,898 pairs; generalized to AND with 100% flip rate on 4,719 pairs). The Transformer baseline reaches equivalent correctness through a qualitatively different representational trajectory (contraction then expansion), suggesting that modular and monolithic architectures implement distinct compositional strategies.
