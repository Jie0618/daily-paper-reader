## 摘要
Raz, Shalyt, Leibtag, Kalisch, Weinbaum, Hadad 和 Kaminer 最近表明，π 的公式可以通过规范多项式递推关系进行组织，并由秩为 2 的守恒矩阵场（CMF）部分统一。我们证明了其论文公开附录 B.6 中明确列出的每个三阶递推关系都是一个显式二阶核的移位求和提升（shifted summation lift），并识别了全部三个核：两个 π 核是散在类 Apéry 序列 A036917 和 A002895（Domb 数，情形 (α)）的显式重定标，而 Catalan 核是 Gauss 平方系数序列在 (a,b,c)=(1/2,1,3/2) 处的超几何扭曲。我们将这些核置于统一的 Sym^2 框架中：第一个 π 核和 Catalan 核直接源自 Gauss 平方系数序列，而 Domb 核则是通过用 CMF 语言重构经典的 3 次 Belyi 拉回 φ(x)=108x^2/(1-4x)^3 及其相关的代数扭曲而得到的。我们为 Gauss CMF 编写了一个显式的平方规范矩阵（square-gauge matrix），用 CMF 术语表述了标准的拉回-扭曲传输，并证明了对于秩为 2 的对象，它与 Sym^2 兼容。我们进一步证明了一个逆分类：对于固定的 Sym^2 型 Riemann 方案，单参数 Fuchs 算子族包含唯一的 Sym^2(Gauss) 点，该点由附加参数（accessory parameter）上的闭式条件 λ_0=2γ_1γ_2(1-2α) 确定。最后，通过对 5040 种配置进行 Belyi 拉回扫描，产生了 11 个形式为 [x^n]λ^n 2F1(a,b;c;φ(x))^2 的额外整数序列；我们证明了它们的整性，并将其置于相同的 Sym^2 拉回框架中。

## Abstract
Raz, Shalyt, Leibtag, Kalisch, Weinbaum, Hadad, and Kaminer recently showed that formulas for $π$ can be organized by canonical polynomial recurrences and partially unified by a rank-$2$ Conservative Matrix Field (CMF). We prove that each order-$3$ recurrence explicitly printed in the public Appendix~B.6 of their paper is a shifted summation lift of an explicit order-$2$ kernel, and identify all three kernels: the two $π$-kernels are explicit rescalings of the sporadic Apéry-like sequences $A036917$ and $A002895$ (Domb numbers, case~$(α)$), while the Catalan kernel is a hypergeometric twist of the Gauss-square coefficient sequence at $(a,b,c)=(\tfrac12,1,\tfrac32)$. We place these kernels in a unified $\operatorname{Sym}^2$ framework: the first $π$-kernel and the Catalan kernel come directly from Gauss-square coefficient sequences, while the Domb kernel is recovered by recasting the classical degree-$3$ Belyi pullback $φ(x)=108x^2/(1-4x)^3$ and the associated algebraic twist in CMF language. We write an explicit square-gauge matrix for the Gauss CMF, formulate the standard pullback--twist transport in CMF terms, and show that for rank-$2$ objects it is compatible with $\operatorname{Sym}^2$. We further prove an inverse classification: for a fixed $\operatorname{Sym}^2$-type Riemann scheme, the one-parameter family of Fuchsian operators contains a unique $\operatorname{Sym}^2(\mathrm{Gauss})$ point, cut out by the closed-form condition $λ_0=2γ_1γ_2(1-2α)$ on the accessory parameter. Finally, a Belyi-pullback scan over $5040$ configurations produces $11$ additional integer sequences of the form $[x^n]λ^n\,{}_2F_1(a,b;c;φ(x))^2$; we prove their integrality and place them in the same $\operatorname{Sym}^2$-pullback framework.
